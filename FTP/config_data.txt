change_password_count maxvalue 32767 (smallint 2^15 = 32768)
If it further increases, its value will not incremented and it will remain on 32767 (In case of forgot_password repeat request)


register_error
---------------
1) Passcode not matching: It will keep increasing count
2) Passcode expired. Please contact admin.: When max count reached

create_password_error
----------------------
1) Passcode not matching: It will keep increasing count

create_password allowed if, previous method is [forgot_password, create_password_error]
